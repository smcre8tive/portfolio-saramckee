My Story

I am a Texas-based digital designer with an art education background & a passion for creative visual problem solving.

I transitioned into UX Design when I learned how this growing field offers authentic insights into human nature and champions design decisions based on these insights.

I believe in the power of education and a growth-mindset. As a neurodiverse individual with ADHD, I offer a unique persepctive. I am an advocate of social justice, social emotional learning, & inclusive design. I work regularly towards growth related to my personal biases, empathy and ADHD self-support strategies.

As a life-long learner, I have a unique blend of visual product management, UX design, UI Design, & User Research expertise.

These skills are supported by an in-progress Master's degree at SCAD in Interaction Design and a variety of other human centered visual design related certifications.

My goal is to help non-profit or B-corporation clients develop digital products that empower and educate the humans that use them.

I look forward to collaborating on projects that drive positive, real-world change.
